Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 17, 2026

Roomel Studio ("the Developer") respects your privacy in relation to Roomel ("the App") and handles information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

The App may collect the following information to provide and improve the App, investigate issues, serve advertisements, and provide in-app purchase functionality.

  • App usage information
  • Theme viewing activity
  • Gacha usage information
  • Advertisement viewing activity
  • In-app purchase activity
  • In-app interaction data
  • Device information
  • Crash information
  • Advertising identifiers

The App does not directly collect personally identifiable information from users, such as name, address, phone number, or email address.

2. Purpose of Use

Information collected by the App is used for the following purposes:

  • Providing and operating the App
  • Analyzing usage trends
  • Investigating issues and crashes
  • Improving service quality
  • Serving advertisements and measuring advertising performance
  • Providing in-app purchase functionality
  • Synchronizing game data and supporting data transfer between devices

3. Third-Party Services

The App may use the following third-party services. Information collected by these services is handled according to their respective privacy policies.

Google AdMob

Used for advertising.

Firebase Analytics

Used to analyze usage and improve the App.

Firebase Crashlytics

Used to collect crash reports and improve application stability.

Google Play Games Services

Used for game data synchronization and data transfer between devices.

Google Play Billing

Used to provide in-app purchase functionality.

4. App Data

Game data is primarily stored locally on the user's device.

If Google Play Games Services is used, some game data may be synchronized or stored through Google's services.

Data may be lost due to app deletion, device replacement, device failure, operating system updates, software bugs, network issues, or other circumstances.

5. Sharing Information

The Developer will not share collected information with third parties except when required by law.

However, third-party service providers described in this Policy may collect information through their services.

6. Children's Privacy

The App is intended for a general audience.

The Developer does not knowingly collect personally identifiable information directly from children under the age required by applicable law.
